PROBLEM
Problem arises with measuring the max width and setting the child div manually.
As you can see above, the child div was on the center by setting the margin-top: 170px; and margin-left: 350px;. Measuring the margins was a common mistake of beginners.
SOLUTION
Instead of using margins to center a div. Use Display: flex or Display: grid and then align-items: center; and justify-content: left;. These 3 properties will center the child div in any viewports.
